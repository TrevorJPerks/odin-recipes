Cashew Cheese Sauce Recipe

Cashew Cheese Sauce

A vegan alternative to Queso cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of raw Cashews
  • 1 clove Garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast flakes
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Sriracha

Steps

  1. Add Cashews to bowl and cover with hot water. Allow the cashews to soak until the water cools to room temp.
  2. Strain the soaked Cashews and add them to a high powered blender with the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Blend on high until smooth. Taste and add more of anything desired.
  4. Store refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 5 days
