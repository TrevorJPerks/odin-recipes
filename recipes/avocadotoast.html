Avocado Toast Recipe

Avocado Toast

It is just Avocado Toast.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Toasted Bread
  • 1 Ripe Avocado
  • Vegan Mayonnaise
  • Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Split the Avocado with a knife, carefully remove the pit, spoon out flesh into a bowl, and mash using a fork.
  2. Spread a thin layer of Vegan Mayonnaise on both slices of toasted bread.
  3. Divide mashed Avocado in half and spread on each slice of toasted bread evenly.
  4. Finish with ground black pepper, and enjoy!
