Spicy Noodle Recipe

Korean style Spicy Noodles

A quick and easy vegan fire noodle dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 brick of Ramen Noodles
  • 1 tbsp Gochujang
  • 1 tbsp Gochugaru
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tsp Sriracha Sauce
  • 1 tsp Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 1 tsp Creamy Peanut Butter
  • 1 2-finger pinch of M.S.G
  • 1 clove of Garlic micro planed
  • Optional: Toasted Sesame Seeds (for garnish)

Steps

  1. To make the sauce, combine Gochujang, Gochugaru, Soy Sauce, Sriracha, Toasted Sesame Oil, Peanut Butter, Garlic, and M.S.G. in a bowl.
  2. In a pot cook your Ramen Noodles until ~90 seconds from being done. Reserve 1/4 cup of noodle water and strain.
  3. Combine the reserved noodle water, and sauce in the pot, and cook over medium heat until well incorporated and peanut butter is melted.
  4. Remove from heat, add noodles to the sauce, and stir to coat. (the noodles will finish cooking during this step)
  5. Transfer to a bowl and serve garnished with toasted sesame seeds.
